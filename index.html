LexiconQuest

Sprint through the staircase of knowledge

Instructions:

  1. Look for posters with a stack of three books in the library.
  2. Scan the stack of three books with the artcodes app provided.
  3. A wordsearch game appears. You have to search for a word in the grid (The wordsearch grid reshuffles every 2 minutes.).
  4. Look for the word through your phone's camera in the library. For example- If your word was apple, you have to look for an apple thorugh your phone's camera.
  5. The word will appear on multiple stacks at multiple locations.
  6. You have to form a sentence using atleast one book from each stack wherever the word has appeared.
  7. Once you have formed the sentence, go to the end of the inner staircase, at its topmost.
  8. You will find rest of the instructions there.

All the best!
